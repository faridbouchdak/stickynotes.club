# Product Design Notes

## Purpose

This document captures observations, discoveries, ideas and open questions made while designing StickyNotes.club.
It is a living document and may change frequently.

---

# Product Philosophy

## StickyNotes.club is not about sticky notes

Sticky notes are the interface.

The product is about:

> **Helping ideas grow.**

---

## The lifecycle of an idea

```text
Capture
    ↓
Share
    ↓
Discuss
    ↓
Organize
    ↓
Collaborate
    ↓
Execute
```

This lifecycle should become visible throughout the product experience.

---

## Public and Private are phases

Public boards are for:

- inspiration
- discovery
- discussion

Private boards are for:

- collaboration
- execution

They are not separate products but different stages of the same journey.

---

# Documentation

## Documentation is product design

The Help Center is simultaneously:

- user documentation
- product specification
- UX documentation
- marketing foundation

---

## Write for user goals

Prefer:

> Organize your work

Instead of:

> Tags & due dates

Users think in tasks, not features.

---

## FAQ is a feedback tool

Every recurring question should trigger one of three actions:

- improve the interface;
- improve documentation;
- build a missing feature.

---

# UX

## Keep It Super Simple (KISS)

Documentation should be:

- short;
- calm;
- practical;
- easy to scan.

---

## Trust users

Start from trust instead of restriction.

Explain the desired behaviour before listing limitations.

---

# Marketing

The Help Center already contains the foundation for the marketing website.

Recurring themes:

- Help ideas grow.
- Share ideas.
- Build ideas together.
- Organize your work.

---

# Process

Current workflow:

1. Build V1 quickly.
2. Reflect.
3. Distill patterns.
4. Improve V2.
5. Update the Playbook.
