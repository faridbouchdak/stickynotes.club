# Product Design Notes

## Current discoveries

-   Sticky notes are the interface, not the product.
-   Documentation became product specification.
-   Marketing naturally emerged from documentation.
-   Public and Private are two phases of one journey.

## Open design questions

-   Can notes be edited?
-   Can notes exist on multiple boards?
-   Should colours carry meaning?
-   How should Public → Private transition work?
-   What is the lifecycle of collaboration?
-   Which functionality belongs in the free plan?

## Future ideas

-   AI-assisted organisation.
-   Better transitions between inspiration and collaboration.
-   Public boards as organic distribution.
-   Reuse documentation for onboarding and AI support.

Rule: ideas live here until they become product decisions.
