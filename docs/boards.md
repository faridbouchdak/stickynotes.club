# Private boards

Boards are your private space for sticky notes — for brainstorms, retrospectives, planning, or just your own thoughts. Boards live at unguessable links, so only people you give access can see them.

## Creating a board

Go to **Boards** and create a new board. Free members get **1 solo board** just for themselves. Premium members can create multiple boards (currently up to 5) and collaborate with others.

## Layouts

Each board has one of two layouts, chosen by the board owner:

* **Grid** — a free-flowing wall of notes
* **Columns** — named columns (like a kanban board), great for workflows such as "To do / Doing / Done" or retro formats like "Went well / To improve"

The owner can add, rename, and delete columns at any time.

## Backgrounds

Board owners can pick a background from a set of presets to give each board its own look.

## Drag & drop

Reorder notes by dragging them — within the grid, or between columns. Anyone with edit rights can reorder; the order is saved automatically for everyone.

## Adding notes

Use the **Add note** form on the board. Premium members can also attach a photo to a board note (photos can't be changed after posting) and set [tags and due dates](tags-and-due-dates.md).

## What happens if my Premium lapses?

Your boards and notes are never deleted. Your oldest board(s) within the free limit stay editable; the rest become read-only and their share links stop working until you reactivate Premium.
