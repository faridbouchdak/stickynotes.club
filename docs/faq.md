# Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for?

Here are answers to some of the questions we hear most often.

---

## Do I need an account?

Yes.

You'll need an account to create sticky notes, collaborate on private boards and manage your profile.

---

## Is StickyNotes.club free?

Yes.

You can use StickyNotes.club for free.

Additional features are available through paid subscriptions.

Learn more in [Plans & subscriptions](/plans-and-subscriptions/).

---

## Can I change my subscription later?

Yes.

You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your subscription from your account settings.

---

## Can I edit or delete my content?

Yes.

You stay in control of the content you create.

The available options depend on where your content is shared.

---

## I can't find the answer to my question.

Visit our [Contact & support](/contact-and-support/) page and we'll be happy to help.
