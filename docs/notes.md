# Sticky notes

The heart of StickyNotes.club: the public wall where everyone's notes live together.

## Posting a note

Click **Create note**, write your message, pick a color, and post it. Free members can post up to **2 notes per day** with the classic colors; Premium members get up to **12 notes per day** and the full premium pastel palette.

## Instant photos (Premium)

Premium members can attach an instant photo with a colorful frame to their note. Photos are processed into a square format automatically.

## Likes and sharing

* **Like** any note by clicking the heart — your liked notes are collected on your Wall of Likes.
* **Share** a note to spread it further.

## Notes by country

Browse the worldwide wall by country at [stickynotes.club/c](https://stickynotes.club/c). Each country has its own page showing notes shared from there.

## Editing and deleting

You can edit or delete your own notes at any time from the note's page.
