# Organize your work

As your board grows, keeping ideas organised becomes just as important as capturing them.

Use tags, colours and due dates to group related ideas, highlight priorities and keep everyone focused on what matters most.

---

## What you'll learn

In this guide you'll learn how to:

- organise sticky notes;
- use tags;
- set due dates;
- keep your board easy to navigate.

---

## Use tags

Tags help you group related ideas.

Create tags that match the way your team works, such as topics, departments, milestones or priorities.

A single sticky note can belong to multiple tags.

---

## Set due dates

Due dates make it clear when something needs attention.

Use them for deadlines, milestones or reminders so everyone knows what comes next.

---

## Keep your board organised

There is no single correct way to organise a board.

Some teams work by project.

Others organise by priority, topic or workflow.

Choose the structure that makes sense for your team and keep it simple.

---

## Review your board regularly

Ideas change over time.

Remove outdated notes, update tags when needed and archive completed work to keep your board focused and easy to use.

---

## Good to know

- Tags make boards easier to browse.
- Due dates help teams stay on schedule.
- Keep tag names short and consistent.
- A simple board is easier to maintain than a complex one.
