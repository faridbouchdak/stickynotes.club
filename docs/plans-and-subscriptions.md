# Plans & subscriptions

StickyNotes.club is free to use.

When you need more ways to capture, organise and personalise your ideas, you can upgrade at any time.

---

## What you'll learn

In this guide you'll learn how to:

- choose the right plan;
- upgrade or change your subscription;
- manage your payments;
- cancel your subscription.

---

## Which plan is right for you?

### Free

Perfect for exploring StickyNotes.club and sharing your first ideas.

Includes:

- Up to 2 sticky notes per day
- Classic sticky note colours
- Like and share public sticky notes

---

### Premium

For people who use StickyNotes.club every day.

Includes everything in Free, plus:

- Up to 12 sticky notes per day
- Premium pastel colours
- Instant photos with colourful frames

**Price:** €4.99 per month (excluding VAT)

---

### Chosen Few

Our most exclusive membership.

Includes everything in Premium, plus:

- Unlimited sticky notes
- Exclusive profile location options
- Membership of the Chosen Few club

**Price:** €999,999.99 (one-time purchase)

---

## Change your subscription

You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

Changes take effect according to your current billing period.

---

## Billing

Your subscription is renewed automatically until you cancel it.

Invoices and payment information are available from your account.

---

## Cancel your subscription

You can cancel your subscription whenever you like.

After cancellation, your paid features remain available until the end of your current billing period.

---

## Good to know

- You can start with the Free plan.
- Upgrading only takes a few moments.
- Your subscription can be managed from your account.
- Your data remains available according to your subscription and account status.
