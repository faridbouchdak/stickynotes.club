# Plans & billing

## Free — € 0

Perfect for getting started:

* Post up to 2 sticky notes per day with the classic colors
* Like and share notes
* 1 private board, just for you

## Premium — € 4.99/month (ex. VAT)

Everything in Free, plus:

* Post up to 12 sticky notes per day
* Premium pastel note colors
* Instant photos with colorful frames — on public notes and board notes
* Multiple private boards (currently up to 5) with full collaboration: email invites, share links, and dot-voting
* Tags and due dates on board notes

## Chosen Few ✨ — one-time upgrade

The ultra-exclusive tier, on top of a Premium subscription. Chosen Few status is permanent once purchased.

## Billing

Payments are processed by [Creem](https://www.creem.io/). Manage your subscription — update payment details, view invoices, or cancel — via the **customer portal** in your account.

## What happens when Premium ends?

Nothing is deleted. You fall back to the Free tier: your oldest board(s) within the free limit stay editable, other boards become read-only, and their share links stop working. Reactivate Premium and everything comes back.
