# Private boards

Private boards are where ideas become plans.

While public sticky notes are meant to inspire and start conversations, private boards give you a dedicated space to collect ideas, work together and turn them into something real.

Only the people you invite can access a private board.

---

## What you'll learn

In this guide you'll learn how to:

* create a private board;
* invite other people;
* organize ideas;
* manage access;
* archive or delete a board.

---

## Create a private board

Create a board whenever you want a dedicated place to work on an idea.

Whether you're planning a project, organizing an event, preparing a workshop or simply collecting inspiration, every board starts empty and grows with your ideas.

1. Select **New board**.
2. Give your board a name.
3. Choose who can access it.
4. Start adding sticky notes.

Your board is immediately ready to use.

---

## Invite people

Boards are designed for collaboration.

Invite colleagues, friends, classmates or anyone else you want to work with.

Everyone you invite sees the same board and can contribute according to the permissions you give them.

---

## Organize your ideas

Move sticky notes around until your board reflects the way your team thinks.

Group related ideas together, separate priorities and let your board evolve naturally as your project develops.

There is no single correct layout.

Your board should match the way your team works.

---

## Manage access

You stay in control of your board.

Add or remove members whenever needed and adjust permissions as your collaboration changes.

Private boards are only visible to invited members.

---

## Archive or delete a board

When a project is finished, you can archive the board to keep your workspace organised while preserving its contents.

If you no longer need the board, you can permanently delete it.

---

## Good to know

* Private boards are not visible on the public wall.
* Only invited members can access a board.
* Every board starts empty and grows with your ideas.
* You can collaborate in real time.
