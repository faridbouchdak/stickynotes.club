# Tags & due dates

Organize your board notes with free-form tags and due dates. Setting them is a Premium feature; viewing and filtering is open to everyone with access to the board.

## Tags

Add up to **5 tags** per board note. Tags are free-form: type anything using letters, numbers, spaces, hyphens, or underscores — they're normalized to lowercase automatically.

Use the **filter bar** at the top of a board to show only the notes with a specific tag. Great for slicing a busy board by topic, person, or theme.

## Due dates

Give a board note a due date to track deadlines. Each note shows its status at a glance:

* **Upcoming** — the due date is in the future
* **Today** — due today
* **Overdue** — the due date has passed
